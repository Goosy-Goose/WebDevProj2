Persona 3: Reload - released feb 2

I have 21 hours on it and it came out three days ago. It's just that good!

Persona 3: Reload is the third re-release of Persona 3. As in, there are four separate games of the same game.

Despite having the number 3 in the name, this game is self standing and easy to pick up on, as most games in the series are disconnected and only share basic premises.

The UI is phenomenal and the gameplay is smooth. The storyline is also incredibly engaging.
